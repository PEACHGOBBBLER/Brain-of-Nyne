ONE BAD CAT

It's been 140 years since felines developed higher intelligence in the United States. Political movements and progressive parties led to them being constituted as a new "race" in the humanitarian sense, leading to provisional civil rights. At first regarded as near-equals, things soon changed as their physique and mental aptitude enabled their more destructive tendencies. While they used to be considered tax paying citizens, they're now seen as demons and criminals.

Brinkley Cruz is a blue, six-foot-two anthropomorphic cat who works as a courier in the inner cities. He makes his living peddling drugs off the rooftops of his Pennsylvania neighborhood. Bold and unabashedly reckless, he works to satiate his narcotic habits. A lost soul with an excentric gaze—at least he isn't alone.

"One Bad Cat" was conceived in the fall of 2021 under the original name, "One Dangerous Cat". It's characters and narratives we're written and illustrated by Manny L' Maverick. Any other artists are credited in the footnote of the comic's cover and are to receive proper acclaim for their part.
